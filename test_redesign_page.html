Today's data problems
Our solution
Data and its power has become siloed in the multitude of business applications and data sources, meaning its distribution challenges have created a Big Data industry that's not fit for purpose
Access
Valluu provides a single window to access all your data and even external data sources, which allows you to produce insights from the data and to change it, with the required pre-approvals in place
The drive for Big Data has been achieved through unstructured data management, which has resulted in huge quality problems as sources are constantly changing and verification & cleansing have become complex tasks
Quality
Valluu solves the data quality problem by putting access to it in the hands of people who understand its purpose and meaning the most, allowing them to constantly ensure good data hygiene and remove the need for the creation of duplicate data versions
Data management gets stuck in the respective data teams, where half of their time is spent trying to understand or build reliable sources of data
Timeliness
With a no-code user interface, Valluu eliminates wasted time by allowing users to generate insights and data change without the need to wait for complex tasks to be completed by others and for IT infrastructure to be made available
Data sources are generating change at a frequency such that current technologies, people, and data management approaches are often behind the needs of the business
Scalability
Valluu allows scale to continue as needed, as changes at source do not impact the user from achieving their required insights or actions; they simply update the associated rules and logic for the intended use of the data when required
The required amount of data processing in today's businesses has resulted in a complex technology stack that becomes ever more costly to utilise
Cost
Valluu efficiently uses your existing on-premise, cloud, or hybrid infrastructure by cleansing its own footprint as soon as execution is completed and thus ensuring that no residual costs are incurred for finished jobs

Integration
Valluu is an intelligent business data management solution that utilises the latest technologies, without requiring any integration or change to the existing business applications or IT infrastructure. It creates and maintains a meta-data library that powers the user interfaces, such as the drag-and-drop tool for creating new data workloads, finance control access and scheduling, with dashboard-based reporting and visualisation. A configurable API enables integration of direct meta dataflows into existing service management systems, to enable visible governance of change, utilisation, spend & control of existing IT assets.
