What do we mean by a 'Data Workload'?

Automate business execution

A  Data Workload is a series of processes used to utilise or change data from both internal and external data sources in near-real-time. The goal of a Data Workload, is to automate the flow of data between various stages of processing, such as data ingestion, cleaning, transformation, and storage.
