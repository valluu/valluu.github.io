What do we mean by a 'Data Workload'?

Transformation of your data

A Data Workload is a series of processes to your data, such as moving the data from one place to another whilst also transforming it along the way, among other use-cases. The goal of a Data Workload could be to automate the flow of data between various stages of processing, such as ingestion, cleansing, transformation, and storage. Data Workloads can handle large volumes of data and enable organisations to process and analyse data in real-time, making it easier to make informed decisions and execute them.
