Data workload democratisation
Empowering the business
Valuu has been designed for business users, removing the need for IT support to create the business understanding derived from running data workloads.
Valluu is a true intelligent data management solution, created to leverage all possible data sources for the business user. Its codeless data workload creation has a native language interface, enabling the solution to be used even by the most unsophisticated of IT system users, with simple drag-and-drop visual objects. The data workloads are catalogued and stored in a library for democratic use by anyone with the sanctioned profile. Workloads can be scheduled to run, when the user requires, via business tools such as Microsoft Teams, where they will then be notified upon completion and have direct access to the outcome in the shared and personal library.
Business Value Realisation
Pricing Adjustment using Valluu
The Motor Insurance Underwriter needs to understand the changes in the Claim profile of accidents, relative to the distance from home since the Covid pandemic, to be able to increase or decrease premiums to improve renewal rates, at the right risk & profit points.
Having defined the business challenge and understood the outcome goals, the Underwriter creates a new data workload to review the pricing data, and schedules the automated run in Microsoft Teams at 6PM on Friday, when the weekend-price-status are set. The Underwriter then receives a notification to say the data workload was run and the results automatically stored in their library. The findings were then presented to the respective business heads on Monday morning and prices revised Monday afternoon.

Operational value realisation
Direct cost savings
Valluu directly addresses the current complexity areas associated with the execution of the next best business action, enabled through data workload management.

This vastly optimises the use of IT infrastructure, dramatically reducing operations & management costs and critically CO2 emissions.

Solution overview
Leveraging the future, now
Valluu has been designed to leverage the capability of Kubernetes & Cloud Services, whilst having the flexibility to run with on-premise & hybrid IT Infrastructure, straight out of the box, with fully-automated setup.
Valluu is an intelligent business data management solution that utilises the latest technologies, without requiring any integration or change to the existing business applications or IT infrastructure. It creates and maintains a meta-data library that powers the user interfaces, such as the drag-and-drop tool for creating new data workloads, finance control access and scheduling, with dashboard-based reporting and visualisation. A configurable API enables integration of direct meta dataflows into existing service management systems, to enable visible governance of change, utilisation, spend & control of existing IT assets.
