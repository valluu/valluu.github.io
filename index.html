Order Management Use Case
The Order Manager receives a notification that there has been a major accident on one of the delivery routes and 3 out of the 5 drops are not going to be met in the drivers allocated time.
The Order Delivery Manager received a notification of the need to re-route the delivery and re-purpose some of the goods to fulfil new orders that just come in. An automated routine can be run by the managers phone, by scheduling the data workload to be run straight away and the new routing and delivery order information sent to the driver.
Business Value Realisation
Pricing Adjustment Use Case
The Motor Insurance Underwriter needs understand the changes in Claim profile of accidents, relative to the distance from home since the COVID pandemic, to be able to increase or decrease the premiums to improve renewal rates at the right risk & profit points.
Having defined the business challenge and understood the outcome goals, the Underwriter creates a new data workload to review the pricing data, and schedules the automated run in Microsoft Teams at 6PM on Friday, when the weekend-price-status are set. The Underwriter then received a notification to say the data workload was run and automatically stored in their library. The findings were then presented to the respective business heads on Monday morning and prices revised Monday afternoon.

Direct cost savings
Valluu directly address the current complexity areas associated with trying to execute the next best business action, enabled through data workload management.
