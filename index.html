Business value realisation through data workload management in Kubernetes
Intelligent business software

Leveraging the power of Kubernetes

We have built the missing piece of the Kubernetes solution required to create an intelligent business solution, data workloads. The current stateless & stateful workloads create the power to leverage information technologies, such as cloud services, but they stop at the infrastructure capability level.
Valluu data workload management solution creates the ability to utilise the required company data sources and business applications. The solution creates a data change management interface, that consolidates the whole data management lifecycle. It creates & maintains a meta-data library that powers the user interfaces, utilises a drag-and-drop tool for creating new data workloads, with auto-deploy of the required data changes to the source database and ongoing iterative utilisation from a centralised library.
Intelligent business solution design
Enabling the business to dynamically implement data changes
By starting from the viewpoint of the business decision maker wanting to implement their business decision, Valluu is the solution that until now, has only been dreamed about, so significant is it’s impact.
Valluu enables business users decisions to be implemented directly to the customer transactions, by allowing data changes to be applied in real-time. This brings alive the possibility of merging multiple data-sources to monitor business decision impacts, based on the actual customers response to using the data.
New data workloads can be created with Valluu without the need for new code created by the IT function, with automatic deployment to the live information technology. Valluu centralises and makes visible the governance of access, change, systems utilisation & spend controls.
Simplify the journey to the Cloud
Reducing re-factoring complexity
One of the challenges of leveraging Kubernetes and Cloud Services is the re-factoring of business applications. Valluu enables the legacy application current state to be maintained for the data workload transaction management. Even after the migration to Kubernetes and the Cloud, Valluu will provide that data transaction management, that currently has to be fully re-engineered. A lot of these data workload transactions are imbedded in code, some of which is at best complementary code, requiring full understanding of the integration impact or at worst has been built with knowledge that no longer exists, and therefore presents a high risk change profile.
This dramatically reduces hardware and software consumption and most importantly reduces the CO2 emissions, by only using compute available when it is running a data workload and automatically clearing it’s footprint, to retain the maximum amount of available capacity for other services.
This environment creates a high risk aversion change & implementation approach, rightly, which adds significantly to costs and loss of time, that companies cannot afford to absorb. Valluu removes this risk profile, simplifys cloud adoption and reduces associated costs by up to 20%.
Solution overview
Leveraging the future, now
Valluu has been designed to leverage the capability of Kubernetes & Cloud Services, whilst having the flexibility to run with on-premise & hybrid IT Infrastructure, straight out of the box, with fully-automated setup.
Valluu is an intelligent business data management solution that utilises the latest technologies, without requiring any integration or change to the existing business applications or IT infrastructure. It creates and maintains a meta-data library that powers the user interfaces, such as the drag-and-drop tool for creating new data workloads, finance control access and scheduling, with dashboard-based reporting and visualisation. A configurable API enables integration of direct meta dataflows into existing service management systems, to enable visible governance of change, utilisation, spend & control of existing IT assets.

Intelligent business in motion
Motor Insurance Pricing Adjustment
The business is losing renewals for second car insurers to their competitors. Having defined the business challenge and understood the required goals, the Underwriter creates a new data workload to review the pricing data, and schedules the automated run in Microsoft Teams at 6PM on Friday, when the weekend-price-status is set. The Underwriter then receives a notification to say the data workload was run and the results automatically stored in their library.
The findings were then presented to the respective business heads on Monday morning and prices automatically revised in the live system via a follow-up data workload. Marketing & Underwriting then collaboratively created new data workloads to monitor the uptake at the new price-points and scheduled variance of pricing based on individuals currently on their books, who matched the profile of the new-price-adopters.
